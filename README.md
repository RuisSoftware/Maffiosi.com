## What is Maffiosi.com?

Maffiosi.com is a **free browser-based mafia RPG** where you start as a street criminal and climb to the top of the underworld.

- 🏙️ **10+ countries** to operate in
- 🔫 **30+ crimes** — car theft, robberies, smuggling, kidnappings
- 🏠 **Real estate** — buy and upgrade properties
- 👥 **Families** — form crews, execute heists, rule together
- 🎰 **Casino** — Blackjack, Roulette, Poker, Slots, Crash, Mines
- ⚔️ **PvP** — Attack other players, steal their businesses
- 🏆 **Season system** — 6-8 week resets with Hall of Fame prizes

[**Play now at Maffiosi.com**](https://maffiosi.com)
